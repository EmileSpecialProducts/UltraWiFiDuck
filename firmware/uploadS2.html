UltraWiFiDuck

Devices ESP-32 (S2)

This will program the UltraWifiDucksoftware also known as a RubberDucky to the ESP32 S2 device.
It supports USB and bluetooth keyboard and mouse simulation.

The folowing devices are supported.

Device USB Keyboard/mouse Bluetooth Keyboard/mouse
ESP32-S2 Yes No
